OLD BOYS
Once a Riccian, Always a Riccian
Ricci Hall Since established in 1929, has provided leadership within the society we served. Shared by our old boys, it was in Ricci Hall that they learned the most treasurable values, commitment, toughness, responsibilities and brotherhood.

Once... Always .... is just how we perceive the Riccian title. This concept rooted in our heart and based in the brotherhood and loyalty we developed in Ricci Hall. Through the decades, Riccians are always keeping contact with the current residents and the linkage has never been broken.

  PROMINENT MEMBERS



Light our road to one of them
Many of our graduates are leading figures in various fields of the society. In different occasions, such as high table dinner or reunion ball, our old boys will return to share their experiences with the current Riccians. It has always been an enjoyable time for the current Riccians to learn from the "Big Sins" about their success stories. As well, it is so warm that our old boys could always share their memories as residents in Ricci Hall decades ago. The current Riccians are always delighted by their jokes and their interesting student life.
