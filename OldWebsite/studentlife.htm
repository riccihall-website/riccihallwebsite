COMMITTEE MEMBERS OF PAST SESSIONS

 

HALL LIFE

UNITY

Unity, the focus of Ricci Hall and the first element of Ricci Spirit, representing the core value of every Riccians. No matter sports events, cultural events or hall activities, all Riccians participate together.
Famed for our hall is the image perceived by other HKU students that Riccians are so united, in every occasion, in red shirit all Riccians participate together. This kind of unity is based on our fraternity which is life long lasting.

Corridor System

Instead of floor system likes other halls, there are 16 corridors in Ricci Hall, composing of 6, 8 or 10 single rooms in each corridor.

Every corridor is like a small family with different year students, the seniors will take care and concern the juniors. Brothers living in the same corridor is called Dor-mates, each corridor will join intra-hall competitions, eat snack at night and have corridor functions in different occasions.

 

 

HALL CULTURE

Where BOYS become MEN

Apart from Unity, Loyalty and Sacrifice, commitment is also a core value of Ricci Hall, we grow and learn from each others and past Riccians through committing to the hall. Junior hallmates will be taken care in their student life, including both academy, hall life and University life. While our hallmates are advancing, they will take the lead gradually, to concern and lead the juniors and to take up more responsibilities.

It is through this change of role and responsibilities that graduates from Ricci Hall are mature and capable. All these self-esteems could not be learned in lecture rooms or books. Over the past decades, Ricci Hall has provided the society with many prominent leaders in various sectors.

 

 

HALL ACTIVITIES

Gong Fight

This function has been carrying on since several decades ago. Every year we have the Gong Fight with Lady Ho Tung Hall as a traditional joint-hall function. Freshmen Captain and Vice Freshmen Captain lead their fellow freshmen to capture the Gong of Lady Ho Tung Hall

The legend goes as the Gong is to signify supper time in Lady Ho Tung Hall. Several decades ago, some naughty Riccians made a trick by secretly transfering the Gong from Lady Ho Tung Hall to Ricci Hall, after that year, this mission became a traditional function. Every lady in Lady Ho Tung Hall will prepare thousands of water bombs to welcome the brothers from Ricci.

Reunion Ball

The Reunion Ball of Ricci Hall is one of the most formal, traditional social functions. It was first held in 1960s. In organising such a grand, splendid, spectacular two-hundred-thousand-dollar function, Riccians become more proud of themselves and Ricci through a whole year’ preparation. The challenges encountered in organizing the Reunion Ball prove to contribute a lot to Riccians’ brotherhood and maturity.

Champion Day

Champion Day is an occasion to share the achievements of our hallmates. Hallmates are rewarded in term of sports, cultural, service contribution for hall. Celebrities are invited to present prizes to Riccians. Smile, tear, excitement, encouragement and warmth are everywhere on the very day.

Dragon Boat Race

In every Tuen-ng Festival Riccians take part in the Dragon Boat Race. This is a traditional and important Hall function and is as well a very good opportunity for all hallmates to relax after the examination in May. After the race, every Riccian will jump into the water to have a Tuen-ng swim.

High Table Dinner

High table dinner is English college tradition. This regular formal occasions of Ricci Hall, as part of our hall education, honorable guests are invited to the dinner to deliver thought-provoking speeches. To respect the occasion, Riccians are all in formal dressing. Associate Members will also join the feast.

Social Gathering

The Ricci Hall Social Gathering is an annual informal social function held by the freshmen. A tailored theme for the programme is decided every year. Throughout this dancing party, you may meet people from all walks of life. Indeed, it is a golden chance for Riccians to develop their talent, manner and social skills.
