Drama team is the largest cultural team in Ricci Hall. It aims at providing Riccians and associate members with opportunities to explore interests in drama and related stage performances. In Ricci Hall, though sports is the forcus, we play drama and other cultural activities as serious interests as we are always ready to challenge ourselves.
