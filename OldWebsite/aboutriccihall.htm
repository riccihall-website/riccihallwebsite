HISTORY
Founded in 1929 by the Society of Jesus, Ricci Hall is the only catholic hostel in the University of Hong Kong. The old building was demolished in 1966 and the new building was officially opened on 8 December 1967, and was completely renovated in the summer of 1990. It has 120 single rooms.

HALL BASIC STRUCTURE
LOCATION
Ricci Hall is situated at 93 Pokfulam Road, ten minutes walk from the HKU campus. Lady Ho Tung Hall is just next to us, Flora Ho Sports Centre is five-minute walk up Pokfulam Road. Kennedy Town is nearby and all kinds of daily necessities are readily accessible.
HALL STRUCTURE
Ricci Hall is marked by its unique physical structure and interlocking system of the building. 5 residential blocks interlock with each other. This structure enhances the interaction between hallmates, 16 corridors with 6-10 rooms in each corridor. All rooms are single rooms with balconies.
FACILITIES
So different from other halls, in Ricci Hall we have a number of exclusive facilities, such as tennis court, car park, dining hall, library, studio, billiard room, music room, TV room, chapel, washing machines. In each corridor there are refrigerator and cooker. Apart from free LAN network, there are WiFi router installed in every corridor last year, so that every hallmates can enjoy WiFi network.
