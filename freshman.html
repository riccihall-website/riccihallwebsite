Where Boys Become Men

Apart from Unity, Loyalty and Sacrifice, commitment is also a core value of Ricci Hall, we grow and learn from each others and past Riccians through committing to the hall. Junior hallmates will be taken care of in their student life, including both academics, hall life and University life. While our hallmates are advancing, they will take the lead gradually, to concern and lead the juniors and to take up more responsibilities.

It is through this change of role and responsibilities that graduates from Ricci Hall become mature and capable. All these values can't be learned in lecture rooms or books. Over the past decades, Ricci Hall has provided the society with many prominent leaders in various sectors.
